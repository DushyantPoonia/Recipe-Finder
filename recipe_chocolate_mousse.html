Chocolate Mousse

A rich and creamy chocolate dessert that melts in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz dark chocolate, chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 large eggs, separated
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Whipped cream and chocolate shavings for garnish

Steps

  1. In a double boiler, melt chocolate and butter; stir until smooth.
  2. In a bowl, whisk egg yolks and sugar until pale; mix with melted chocolate.
  3. In another bowl, whip cream until soft peaks form; fold into chocolate mixture.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites until stiff peaks form; fold into mousse.
  5. Chill for at least 2 hours before serving; garnish with whipped cream and chocolate shavings.
