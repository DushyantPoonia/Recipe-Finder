Chicken Parmesan

Classic Italian-American dish with breaded chicken and marinara.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, flattened
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Olive oil for frying
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Dip chicken in egg, then coat in breadcrumbs mixed with Parmesan.
  3. Fry chicken in olive oil until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Place in a baking dish; top with marinara and mozzarella.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes until cheese is bubbly; garnish with basil.
