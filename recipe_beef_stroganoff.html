Beef Stroganoff

A creamy beef dish served over egg noodles.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef, sliced into strips
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Egg noodles, for serving

Steps

  1. In a skillet, melt butter and saut√© onions and garlic until translucent.
  2. Add beef strips and cook until browned.
  3. Add mushrooms and cook until tender.
  4. Sprinkle flour over the mixture and stir well.
  5. Add beef broth and simmer until thickened.
  6. Stir in sour cream and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve over cooked egg noodles.
