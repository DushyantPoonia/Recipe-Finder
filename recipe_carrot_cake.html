Carrot Cake

A moist and flavorful cake packed with carrots and spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 3 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
  • Cream cheese frosting for topping

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C); grease and flour two 9-inch round pans.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. Add oil, eggs, carrots, pineapple, and walnuts; mix until well combined.
  4. Divide batter between prepared pans; bake for 25-30 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Cool in pans for 10 minutes; remove and cool completely before frosting.
