Beef Tacos

Delicious and easy beef tacos perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • Sour cream for serving

Steps

  1. In a skillet, cook ground beef until browned; drain excess fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water; simmer until thickened.
  3. Warm taco shells according to package instructions.
  4. Fill shells with beef, lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.
  5. Top with sour cream and serve.
