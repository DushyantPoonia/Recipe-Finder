Apple Pie

A classic dessert made with sweet apples and a flaky crust.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, chilled
  • 6-8 tbsp ice water
  • 6-8 apples, peeled and sliced
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine flour, salt, and sugar.
  3. Add chilled butter and mix until crumbly; stir in ice water.
  4. Divide dough in half, roll out to fit pie dish.
  5. In another bowl, mix apples, sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  6. Place apple mixture in pie crust and cover with the top crust.
  7. Cut slits in the top and bake for 45 minutes.
  8. Let cool before serving.
