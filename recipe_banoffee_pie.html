Banoffee Pie

A delightful dessert made with bananas and toffee.

Ingredients

  • 1 package digestive biscuits
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Grated chocolate for topping

Steps

  1. Crush the biscuits and mix with melted butter; press into a pie dish.
  2. Cook the condensed milk in a pot for about 1 hour until caramelized.
  3. Spread the toffee over the biscuit base; slice bananas and layer on top.
  4. Whip the cream and spread over the bananas; garnish with chocolate.
  5. Chill before serving.
