Butter Chicken

A rich and creamy chicken dish infused with Indian spices.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup cream
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices: garam masala, cumin, coriander
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with spices and yogurt for 30 minutes.
  2. Saut√© onions until golden brown, then add ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until done.
  5. Stir in cream and simmer for a few minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
