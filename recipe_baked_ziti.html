Baked Ziti

A comforting baked pasta dish with cheese and marinara.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz ziti pasta
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook ziti according to package instructions; drain.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, and half of the mozzarella; combine with ziti and marinara.
  4. Transfer to a baking dish; top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes until cheese is bubbly; garnish with basil.
