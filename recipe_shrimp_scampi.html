Shrimp Scampi

Garlic butter shrimp served over pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 oz linguine
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook linguine according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil and butter; add garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp, wine, and red pepper flakes; cook until shrimp is pink.
  4. Toss in linguine; season with salt and pepper.
  5. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.
