Chow Mein

A delicious stir-fried noodle dish.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz chow mein noodles
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, bean sprouts)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Green onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the chow mein noodles according to package instructions; drain.
  2. In a pan, heat oil; add garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add mixed vegetables; stir-fry for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Add noodles and sauces; toss to combine and heat through.
  5. Garnish with green onions before serving.
