Falafel Wrap

Delicious falafel in a wrap with fresh veggies and tahini sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • Pita bread or wraps
  • Vegetables (lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers)
  • Tahini sauce

Steps

  1. In a food processor, blend chickpeas, onion, garlic, spices, salt, and pepper.
  2. Form mixture into balls or patties.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry falafel until golden brown.
  4. Fill pita or wrap with falafel, fresh vegetables, and drizzle with tahini sauce.
  5. Serve immediately.
