Pesto Pasta

A quick and fresh pasta dish with basil pesto.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta of choice
  • 1 cup basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cherry tomatoes for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions; drain and reserve some pasta water.
  2. In a food processor, blend basil, pine nuts, and cheese; drizzle in olive oil.
  3. Add pasta to the pesto; toss to coat, adding reserved water as needed.
  4. Season with salt and pepper; serve with cherry tomatoes on top.
