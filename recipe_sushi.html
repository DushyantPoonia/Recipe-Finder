Sushi

Japanese dish of vinegared rice accompanied by various ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  • Seaweed sheets (nori)
  • Fillings: cucumber, avocado, raw fish (optional)
  • Soy sauce, for serving

Steps

  1. Rinse sushi rice until water runs clear, then cook with water.
  2. Mix rice vinegar with cooked rice and let it cool.
  3. Lay nori on a bamboo mat and spread rice evenly.
  4. Add fillings and roll tightly, then slice into pieces.
  5. Serve with soy sauce.
