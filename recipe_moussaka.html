Moussaka

A traditional Greek casserole made with eggplant and ground meat.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants, sliced
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated cheese for topping

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Salt eggplant slices and let sit for 30 minutes; rinse and pat dry.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil; add onion and garlic, and sauté.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned; stir in crushed tomatoes and cinnamon.
  5. Layer eggplant, meat sauce, and béchamel in a baking dish; top with cheese.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes until golden brown.
