Stuffed Bell Peppers

Colorful bell peppers filled with a savory mixture of rice and meat.

Ingredients

  • 4 bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut tops off peppers and remove seeds; place in a baking dish.
  3. In a skillet, brown meat; add cooked rice, tomatoes, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stuff the mixture into the peppers; top with shredded cheese.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes until peppers are tender and cheese is melted.
