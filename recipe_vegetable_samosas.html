Vegetable Samosas

Spicy and crispy pastries filled with mixed vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Water as needed
  • 2 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, etc.)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, oil, and salt; gradually add water to form a dough.
  2. In another pan, heat oil; add cumin seeds, then add mashed potatoes and mixed vegetables.
  3. Season with garam masala, salt, and pepper; mix well and let cool.
  4. Roll out dough, cut into circles, fill with vegetable mixture, and fold to seal.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan; fry samosas until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
