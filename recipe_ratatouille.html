Ratatouille

A classic French vegetable dish that is healthy and vibrant.

Ingredients

  • 1 eggplant, diced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet; add onion and garlic and saut√© until soft.
  2. Add eggplant and bell pepper; cook until softened.
  3. Add zucchini and tomatoes; season with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.
