Paneer Tikka

Grilled paneer marinated in spices, served with chutney.

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp tikka masala
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into cubes
  • 1 onion, cut into cubes
  • Salt, to taste
  • Skewers

Steps

  1. Marinate paneer with yogurt and spices for 1 hour.
  2. Thread paneer, bell pepper, and onion onto skewers.
  3. Grill the skewers until charred and cooked through.
  4. Serve hot with mint chutney.
