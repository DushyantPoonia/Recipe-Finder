Shrimp Tacos

Spicy shrimp tacos topped with fresh salsa and avocado.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Fresh salsa for topping

Steps

  1. In a bowl, toss shrimp with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat; cook shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side until pink.
  3. Warm tortillas in a separate skillet or microwave.
  4. Assemble tacos with shrimp, cabbage, avocado, and salsa.
  5. Serve immediately.
