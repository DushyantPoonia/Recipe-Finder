Tacos

Mexican dish consisting of a folded tortilla filled with various ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 8 small tortillas
  • 300g ground beef or beans
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • Lettuce, shredded
  • Cheese, grated
  • Salsa, for serving

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef with onions until browned.
  2. Warm tortillas in a pan.
  3. Assemble tacos with beef, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and salsa.
  4. Serve immediately.
