Tom Yum Soup

A spicy Thai soup with shrimp, mushrooms, and aromatic herbs.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, chopped
  • 4-5 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1-2 Thai chilies, smashed
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring broth to a boil in a pot.
  2. Add lemongrass, lime leaves, and chilies; simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Add mushrooms and shrimp; cook until shrimp turns pink.
  4. Stir in fish sauce and lime juice.
  5. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.
