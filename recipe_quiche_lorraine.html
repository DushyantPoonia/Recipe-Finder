Quiche Lorraine

A savory pie filled with eggs, cream, and bacon.

Ingredients

  • 1 pie crust
  • 6 slices bacon, cooked and chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (Gruyère or cheddar)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place pie crust in a pie dish and prick with a fork.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together cream, eggs, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add bacon and cheese to the crust; pour egg mixture on top.
  5. Bake for 35-40 minutes until set and golden.
