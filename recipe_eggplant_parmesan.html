Eggplant Parmesan

A classic Italian dish with layers of eggplant, marinara, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C); grease a baking dish.
  2. Sprinkle salt on eggplant slices; let sit for 30 minutes to draw out moisture.
  3. Rinse and pat dry; dip slices in olive oil and coat with breadcrumbs.
  4. Arrange in baking dish, layering eggplant, marinara, and cheeses.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes until bubbly and golden. Garnish with fresh basil.
