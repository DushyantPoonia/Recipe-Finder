Greek Salad

A refreshing salad with cucumbers, tomatoes, and feta cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 cucumbers, chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 200g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine cucumbers, tomatoes, onion, bell pepper, and olives.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and vinegar; season with salt and pepper.
  3. Toss gently to combine.
  4. Top with crumbled feta cheese before serving.
