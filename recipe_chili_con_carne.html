Chili Con Carne

A hearty and spicy chili made with ground beef and beans.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cans (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (28 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil, for cooking

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and bell pepper; saut√© until softened.
  3. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Add ground beef; cook until browned.
  5. Stir in chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  6. Add diced tomatoes and kidney beans; bring to a simmer.
  7. Reduce heat and let simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Serve hot with your favorite toppings (sour cream, cheese, etc.).
