Pumpkin Soup

A creamy and comforting soup perfect for autumn.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pumpkin puree
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pumpkin seeds for garnish

Steps

  1. In a pot, saut√© onion and garlic until translucent.
  2. Add pumpkin puree and vegetable broth; bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat; simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Stir in heavy cream, nutmeg, salt, and pepper; blend until smooth.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with pumpkin seeds.
