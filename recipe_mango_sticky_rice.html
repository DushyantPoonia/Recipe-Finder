Mango Sticky Rice

A popular Thai dessert made with glutinous rice, coconut milk, and mango.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup glutinous rice
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 ripe mangoes, sliced
  • Sesame seeds or mung beans for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Soak rice in water for 4 hours or overnight; drain.
  2. Steam rice for 20-25 minutes until cooked.
  3. In a saucepan, heat coconut milk, sugar, and salt until dissolved.
  4. Mix 3/4 of the coconut sauce with the cooked rice; let sit for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve rice with mango slices; drizzle with remaining coconut sauce and garnish if desired.
