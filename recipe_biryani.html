Biryani

A fragrant rice dish cooked with spices and meat/vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken or vegetables
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • Spices: bay leaves, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh mint and coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Soak rice in water for 30 minutes and drain.
  2. Saut√© onions until golden brown, then add tomatoes and spices.
  3. Add chicken or vegetables and cook until done.
  4. Add rice and water, bring to a boil, then simmer until rice is cooked.
  5. Garnish with fresh mint and coriander before serving.
