Vegetable Stir Fry

A quick and colorful mix of vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, carrots)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
  • Rice or noodles for serving

Steps

  1. In a wok, heat sesame oil; add garlic and ginger, and saut√©.
  2. Add mixed vegetables; stir-fry for about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Pour in soy sauce and toss to coat.
  4. Serve hot over rice or noodles, garnished with sesame seeds.
