Pasta

A classic Italian dish made with pasta and sauce.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta of choice
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Olive oil, for cooking
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Parmesan cheese, for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions and drain.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© onions and garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add cooked pasta to the sauce and mix well.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with Parmesan cheese.
