Curry Goat

A flavorful Caribbean dish that is slow-cooked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs goat meat, cut into pieces
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1-2 Scotch bonnet peppers
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate goat with curry powder, garlic, ginger, and salt; let sit for 1 hour.
  2. In a pot, heat oil and saut√© onion until soft.
  3. Add goat and cook until browned; add water and peppers.
  4. Cover and simmer for about 2 hours until tender.
  5. Serve with rice or roti.
